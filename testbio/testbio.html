About Me

My name is Cody Crozier. I am a chemist, biologist, and aspiring web developer. I graduated from Northern Arizona University in 2016, where I studied chemistry and biology. I did some awesome research as an undergraduate working on destroying biofilms! I have also loved working with and on computers since I was little. I am enrolled in the University of Arizona coding bootcamp where I hope to learn the skills to create fluid, user friendly, and aesthetically pleasing webpages. I am extremely excited to start my journey towards becoming a full stack developer!


When I am not coding, I love the outdoors, snowboarding, cooking, craft beer, coffee, and music. My favorite bands are Radiohead, Bon Iver, and Tool. Connect with me if you want to chat about web development, music, or anything else!
