WHO AM I?

I'm a lifelong learner with a thirst for solving tough problems. I began coding on my own and found a passion in web development. I find software engineering to be the science that best utilizes my ability to solve problems and think critically. I live and breathe code and see every day as an opportunity to fine tune my craft. Most days you can find me at the local coffee shop networking, learning, and building software. I want to develop software to help people and improve people's lives.

MY LATEST WORK

CODEx

An educational and networking repository for students learning to code. Allows user to search for a topic they wish to learn more about and presents them with a tutorial on the topic, a codepen to code along, meet-up events in their area, and quizzes to test their knowledge.

Whiteboard

This project was built in 10 days, and is my favorite work. A dynamic syllabus where instructors can create custom courses, and students can enroll to share resources and give feedback.

Science Scraper

An article repository that scrapes a popular science news sites for up to date articles. Users can view an article summary, comment on an article, and visit the site to read the full article.
